Ser Libre

Patricio Rizzo-Vast

Enter Dreamland. Patrico Rizzo-Vast’s art collection, Ser Libre, weaves sentimental collages and poetry, oftentimes focusing on the microcosmic connections of the world. A transient piece through the lens of romantic Zen Buddhism and magic realism.


Genre: Zine / Poetry / Collage /

Artwork & Poetry: Patricio Rizzo-Vast

Design: Timothy Dilich / Maxwell Stern /

Specs: 44 pages / 5" x 4" /

Release: March 2013

Limited Edition: 1st of 100
